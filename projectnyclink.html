Usability Testing on NYC park website

For my User Research and Data Analytics class, I conducted usability tests on the NYC park website. I carefully observed and recorded user interactions, collected qualitative feedback, and analyzed quantitative data to gain valuable insights into the website's strengths and areas that required improvement. By combining user observation with data analytics techniques, I extracted meaningful patterns and trends from the collected data to inform my recommendations.
