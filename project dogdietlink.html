Dogdiet App Prototype

The "DogDiet" project was undertaken with the objective of conducting a thorough evaluation of the official online platform for the innovative dog diet app, known as "DogDiet." The primary focus was to critically assess the app's user interface, information architecture, and visual design in order to identify opportunities for improvement and refinement.
