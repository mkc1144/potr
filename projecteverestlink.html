Climbing Mount Everest: A VR Experience

As part of my Human Interaction Design class, I undertook an exciting project to create a virtual reality (VR) experience called "Climbing Mount Everest." Utilizing my skills in Unity, I developed an immersive and educational journey that allows users to simulate the challenging ascent of Mount Everest.
