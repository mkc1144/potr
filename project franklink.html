Redesign Analysis on Frank Ocean's Website

The objective of this project was to critically evaluate the official online platform for the renowned artist Frank Ocean, known as "blonded.co." Through a meticulous assessment, I examined various aspects of the website's user interface, information architecture, and visual design to identify areas for enhancement.
